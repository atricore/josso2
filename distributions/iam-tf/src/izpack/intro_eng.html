Simplified Identity and Access

IAM.tf is an open source Internet identity and access management solution for rapid and standards-based Internet-scale Single Sign-On implementations, allowing secure Internet access to the Web-based applications or services of customers, suppliers, and business partners.

Featuring :

  • All-In-One Identity Management Solution
  • Point-and-Click Setup and Administration
  • Standards-based : SAML compliance
  • Professionally Supported Enterprise Edition
